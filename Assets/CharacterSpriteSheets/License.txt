This asset pack contains one hero, with 6 animation cycles for various actions. The pack also comes with 4 enemy types with a bunch of animation cycles, a hand full of props, and layered background art. Not to forget the UI kit with 9 slice buttons, HUD elements, and game over title. The asset can be easily imported into Unity or an open-source game engine like Superpowers. These assets were originally made for 32nd Ludum Dare, and now it's free available for your hop-and-bop side-scroll project. The assets are free and not licensed to any commercial project.
